Q1 2024 Executive Summary

Introduction:
This report covers the first quarter of 2024 performance across all business units. We saw significant growth in multiple areas and some challenges that need addressing.

Revenue Performance:
Total revenue reached $5.2M, representing a 15% year-over-year increase. This growth was driven primarily by strong performance in the electronics category and exceptional results from our mobile app channel.

Channel Analysis:
Our mobile app channel was the star performer this quarter, growing at an impressive 30% rate. Desktop website traffic remained stable at 2% growth. In-store sales declined slightly by 5%, likely due to seasonal factors and increased preference for digital shopping.

Customer Metrics:
Customer retention improved to 85%, up from 78% last quarter. New customer acquisition cost decreased by 12%, making our marketing spend more efficient. Average order value increased by 8%.

Product Category Performance:
Electronics dominated sales with 45% of total revenue. Furniture came in second at 28%, while office supplies represented 15% and appliances 12% of sales respectively.

Marketing Initiatives:
Our Q1 marketing campaigns focused heavily on mobile-first strategies. Push notifications drove 23% of mobile app sales. Email campaigns saw open rates of 31%, above industry average.

Operational Improvements:
We reduced fulfillment time from 48 hours to 36 hours on average. Customer support response times improved by 40%. Returns decreased by 15% due to better product descriptions.

Challenges Faced:
Supply chain delays affected 8% of orders in January and February. Competition in the electronics space intensified. Mobile app development costs exceeded budget by 18%.

Q2 Recommendations:
- Continue investing in mobile app features and user experience
- Expand electronics product line with new suppliers
- Launch customer loyalty program with tiered benefits
- Improve in-store experience to compete with digital channels
- Optimize supply chain with additional vendor partnerships

Conclusion:
Q1 2024 was a strong quarter overall. The mobile channel success validates our digital-first strategy. We must address supply chain challenges and control development costs while maintaining growth momentum.
